Human Physiology

Human physiology is the scientific study of the functions and mechanisms in a human body. It includes the study of how organs and systems work together to sustain life.

Homeostasis

Homeostasis is the body's ability to maintain a stable internal environment despite changes in external conditions. It involves various feedback mechanisms to regulate temperature, pH, and other vital parameters.

Cell Function

Cells are the basic units of life. They perform essential functions such as energy production, waste removal, and reproduction. Specialized cells form tissues and organs, each with specific roles.

Organ Systems

Organ systems, such as the cardiovascular, respiratory, and digestive systems, work together to ensure the body's proper functioning. Each system has unique physiological processes that contribute to overall health.
